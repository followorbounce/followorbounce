## Why Contracts Matter

The agreements that govern digital life — terms of service, open source licences, platform policies, data-sharing deals — are among the most consequential documents most people never read.

We read them. Then we explain what they actually mean.

## Our Sections

### [Digital Law](/contracts/digital-law/)
Legislation, regulation, and case law that shapes how technology operates. GDPR, CCPA, the DSA — the rules are changing fast. We track what matters.

### [Open Source](/contracts/open-source/)
Licences, governance models, and the legal foundations of collaborative software. From MIT to AGPL, from Apache to Commons Clause.

### [Platform Terms](/contracts/platform-terms/)
The terms of service and policies that govern the platforms we all use. What they say, what they mean, and what changed in the latest update.

## Our Method

We are not lawyers. We are careful readers who work with legal advisors to ensure accuracy. Our plain-language breakdowns are designed to be *accessible*, not *authoritative* — always consult a qualified solicitor for legal advice.
