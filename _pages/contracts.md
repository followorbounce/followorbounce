## Why This Section Exists

Every piece of software you use is wrapped in legal language
you almost certainly have never read. We do the reading for you —
and translate what it actually means for how you live and work.

### What We Cover

**Digital Law** — Copyright, data protection, platform liability, and the statutes shaping the internet.

**Agreements** — Terms of service, employment contracts, and the fine print that matters.

**Frameworks** — GDPR, DMCA, Creative Commons, and the regulatory structures behind them.
