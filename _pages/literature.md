## What We Read

We believe slow reading is a radical act. The Literature section covers
the books, essays, and ideas that hold up under scrutiny — not trend pieces,
not listicles, not airport thrillers.

### Categories

**Essays** — Original long-form writing from the Follow or Bounce team and contributors.

**Reviews** — Honest assessments of new and classic works, written for readers, not algorithms.

**Long-form** — Extended deep-dives that take the time an idea actually needs.
