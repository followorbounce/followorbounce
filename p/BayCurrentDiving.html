Logo Mark — Explained

Hull arc — the curved line represents a boat hull cross-section viewed from below the waterline. Waterline — the horizontal Current-blue line separates above from below. Vertical drop — the diver descending to work. Signal dot — the surface marker buoy: orange, visible, precise. Works at 16px (favicon) through billboard. Embroidery: use hull arc + signal dot only at small sizes.

07 — Workwear & Decals

Embroidery

Use wordmark only: BAY CURRENT / DIVING in Syne 800, two lines, chest-left patch. Logo mark works as standalone badge. Signal orange thread on charcoal or navy. Never embroider the full lockup at under 40px equivalent.

Boat Hull Decal

White on dark, high contrast. Single line: BAY CURRENT DIVING · baycurrentdiving.com · (209) 552-6958. Logo mark at left. DM Mono for contact info. No colour — white only. Cut vinyl preferred over print.

Truck / Vehicle

Rear window: large BAY CURRENT DIVING in Syne across full width. Phone number in DM Mono below. White on dark tint. Door panels: logo mark + wordmark only, no taglines.

Clothing Palette

Charcoal Carhartt work shirts. Navy or black waders. Black coveralls. Signal orange as accent stripe or patch only. No bright prints. No loud graphics. Workwear that looks right on a dock.

Equipment Stickers

Tanks, cases, toolboxes: logo mark + BAY CURRENT DIVING in one line. Black or white depending on surface. Domed vinyl or anodised metal plate for marine environment durability.

Business Card

Ink background. Logo top-left. Name: Tony in Syne 60px. Role in DM Mono. Phone, SMS, email as three lines DM Mono. Matte laminate, 400gsm minimum. No rounded corners.

08 — SEO & Digital Guidelines

Target Keywords

hull cleaning San Francisco underwater diver SF Bay barnacle removal Bay Area zinc replacement diving Fisherman's Wharf boat service commercial fishing hull cleaning prop inspection San Francisco underwater hull maintenance

Animation Rules

Fade-up on scroll. 0.7s ease. Stagger cards by 80ms max. No parallax — performance impact on mobile. Nav blur activates at scrollY > 20px. Process steps animate on section entry. No autoplay video.

Mobile Behaviour

Sticky "Call Tony" replaces nav links on mobile. Services: 2-col grid. Hero type: clamp() fluid scale. Process: vertical stack. Contact methods: 48px+ tap targets. No horizontal scroll.
