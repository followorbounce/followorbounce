## What We Cover

Technology moves fast. Our coverage moves deliberately. We focus on:

- **Software & infrastructure** — architecture decisions, tradeoffs, and the engineering culture behind them
- **AI & machine learning** — beyond the hype, into the practical and the philosophical
- **Developer tools** — what actually improves how we build things, and why
- **Digital economics** — the business models and incentive structures driving the platforms we use

## Our Approach

We're not a news aggregator. If a story appeared in ten other places yesterday, we'll only cover it if we have something genuinely new to add — a different angle, deeper context, or a contrarian read.

We interview practitioners, not just executives. We read the documentation, not just the press releases.
