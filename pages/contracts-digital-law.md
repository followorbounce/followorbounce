## The Regulatory Landscape

Digital regulation is accelerating. After decades of light-touch oversight, governments worldwide are enacting legislation that directly affects how technology companies operate, how data is handled, and what rights users have.

---

## Key Frameworks We Track

**GDPR** — The EU's General Data Protection Regulation remains the global benchmark for data privacy law.

**CCPA / CPRA** — California's privacy framework has become the de facto US standard.

**Digital Services Act** — The EU's DSA imposes significant new obligations on large platforms, from content moderation to algorithmic transparency.

**AI Act** — The EU's risk-based framework for artificial intelligence systems, now reshaping AI development across the industry.
