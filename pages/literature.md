## What We Read

Literature, for us, is anything written with intention. That includes novels and essays, but also manifestos, policy papers, and the occasional well-crafted thread. We care about *how* ideas are communicated as much as *which* ideas.

---

## What We Review

- **Long-form essays** — from publications that prioritise depth
- **Books** — particularly at the edges of technology, culture, and society
- **Ideas** — when a concept becomes culturally important, we examine it
- **Writing craft** — occasionally, we write about writing itself

---

## Our Critical Standard

We don't publish summaries. A review from Follow or Bounce will tell you what a book *does*, not just what it's *about*. We're interested in argument, structure, and implication.

We're also honest about books we think are overrated. A publication that praises everything is a publication that means nothing.
